    One of the most challenging parts of my college journey was working on our research title. There were times when I felt lost or unsure about the direction of our study, especially when it came to gathering sources, writing technical content, and aligning everything with our objectives. Through it all, I gained valuable life lessons in perseverance, self-discipline, and emotional resilience.

    Future Plans in Life

    My future plans in life are simple yet meaningful. First and foremost, I hope to build a stable and comfortable life — one where I can support myself and my loved ones without constant worry. I believe that stability comes not only from hard work, but also from wise choices, continuous learning, and surrounding myself with people who uplift and inspire me. Success for me is not just about financial gain — it’s about peace of mind, genuine happiness, and being proud of the person I become.
